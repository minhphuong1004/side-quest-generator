# 🌱 Side Quest Generator

A tiny, cozy web that gives you small, gentle side quests for your day.

Sometimes you don’t need a big goal.  
Just something simple. Something soft. Something doable.

---

## ✨ What it does

Click a button → get a random quest.

Quests are:
- small
- low-pressure
- sometimes a little playful
- sometimes quietly meaningful

Examples:
- “Sit by a window and do nothing for 5 minutes”
- “Write a note to your future self”
- “Pretend you're a character completing a quest log”

---

## 🌷 Why I made this

I wanted a small space on the internet that feels like:
- a pause
- a breath
- a gentle nudge forward

Not productivity.  
Not pressure.  
Just… movement.

---

## 🛠️ Built with

- HTML  

No frameworks, no complexity—just a simple idea.
